First Aid Tips You Should Know

Burns

Cool the burn under running water for 10 minutes. Cover with a sterile, non-stick dressing.

Bleeding

Apply pressure with a clean cloth. Elevate the injured area. Use sterile bandage.

Choking

Use the Heimlich maneuver: thrusts below the ribs to dislodge the blockage.

Fracture

Stabilize the limb with a splint. Avoid moving it and call emergency services.

CPR

Push hard and fast on the chest. 30 compressions, 2 rescue breaths if trained.

Poisoning

Do not induce vomiting. Contact poison control immediately. Provide info.

Heatstroke

Move person to a cool place. Apply cold cloths and hydrate slowly with water.

Nosebleed

Lean forward, pinch the nose for 10 minutes. Avoid tilting head back.
